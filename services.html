Services

Data Protection Officer (DPO) as a Service

The XMG will provide Data Protection Officers (DPO) for organisations who require professional data protection services to demonstrate an enhanced level of compliance or due to the mandority legal requirement. Our DPO's offer advice and guidance, incident plans and regulation assurance.

Security Services

All organisation, no matter size or type, are under threat of data loss, breach, corruption, theft or misuse. The threats to data are on the increase and are high likely to occur unless you take the necessary steps to prevent them for happening.

If a data breach does occur, it can be a very serious and costly issue. According to an industry study by The Diffusion Group, who surveyed small business organisations, 60 percent of companies that lose their data close down within six months of the disaster and a staggering 72 percent of businesses that suffer major data loss disappear within 24 months.

Data Storage Solutions

XMG has transformed state of the art IT infrastructure into a utility with on-demand access via the internet. With data centers located in New Jersey, the XMG hosts their own cloud computing capabilities, providing a plethora of different cloud computing services, as well as using general storage services.

  • Software as a service (SaaS) - Software hosted in the cloud that can be accessed via a web browser, rather than downloading the software.
  • Data Storage and backup - Provide a location for company data to be stored in case of need for backup or restoration.
  • Platform as a service (PaaS) - Provides hardware, software, infrastructure and development tools over the internet
