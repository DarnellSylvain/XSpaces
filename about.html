About X Spaces


Who We Are



X Spaces is a Data Analytics company located in London which specialises in storage, managment, security and protection of data. We are a Minority-Owned Business Enterprise corporation, incorporated in March of 2021 in assosiation with XMG ltd.. It was ounded with an objective of conducting/ providing data research, and data managment B2B services. We operate globally and are based in London, New York and San Francisco. We pride ourselves on our cutting-edge rapid prototyping in which uses vertical manufacturing to assemble product prototypes. This advanced technology enables us to create and recreate product ideas at high fidelity as well as allowing us to identify the most efficient and cost-effective processes for manufacturing. We accomplish this by using Natural Language Processing (Product parameters written in a language which our system can interpret, enabling us to create digital renders of the desired product), Robotics to automate the manufacturing process therefore increasing efficiency and 3D Manufacturing which allows us to reduce the ‘product development cycle’ and aids in identifying areas which can be improved.
