Data Protection Officer

What You Get

Our DPO service includes all the features listed below. The level of this service will depend upon the time required.

Guidance

Incident Plan

Regulation Assurance

Dedicated Officer

We provide advice and guidance on all your data protection issues and enquiries.

We provide advice and guidance by either telephone or email as and when required (note – we endeavour to provide this within 24 hours of initial contact). This crucially includes dealing with inquiries from new and potential customers and assisting with bid management processes.

Our DPOs are involved in providing initial guidance on breaches of data protection and also the handling of Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs). Depending upon the level of service, we often take full accountability for managing this within the organisation.

We develop your incident management plan and manage this in the event of a breach/loss.

In the event of a data breach, the DPO will act as the liason and incident manager. The DPO provivdes the initial validation of the breach and advises the controller of the activities required to manage the incident properly and within regulations. The DPO will ensure that the correct authorities are informared within 72 hours of the breach becoming known to the client organisations.

We will monitor your level of compliance and make appropriate recommendations.

As the GDPR affects U.S companies, they need to adhere to its regulations and practises, XMG can assist businesses’ in becoming fully compliant with GDPR law, providing necessary documents as evidence.

This is an important way of expediting your quality management approach to privacy by design in general, as well as demonstrating compliance to your business partners. The most important benefit of this service is the assurance that areas of risk will be identified and managed before they result in a breach of regulation.

Our DPO's meet regularly with you and keep your team up-to-date.

Your appointed Data Protection Officer will regularly attend your offices on a mutually agreed planned basis to meet with senior management and provide them with with a written report on their current risks and issues. Our objective is to fully integrate our services into your organtion so that we can mutually progress towards implementing a seamless services, indisguinshable from an in-house service
